    About Us

    Arab Home is a federally registered corporation based in Ontario.
    Arab Home is a multi-service hub for the Arab community in Canada and the Arabs who are willing to come to Canada in the future for studying or resettlement.
    We provide a wide range of activities under the umbrella of language solutions and consulting such as certified translation and interpretation services, educational consulting, English language teaching, IELTS preparation classes, etc.
    In partnership with certified immigration consultants, we also provide free immigration-related consultations. We are serving the Arab community to assist them make a smooth transition, proper resettlement and successful integration into Canadian life. We presently manage two locations in Canada and Iraq, and all our efforts are dedicated to serve you and communicate with you in both English and Arabic. Plans are underway to open more locations in the Middle East. Quality is the cornerstone of our commitment to deliver services of the highest quality at competitive pricing within agreed upon deadlines. Our work is not done until the work is approved by our clients and they are completely satisfied. Privacy and Confidentiality are assured.
